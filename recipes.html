  • Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

    Ingredients

    • 12 oz (340g) spaghetti
    • 4 oz (115g) pancetta or bacon, diced
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

    Instructions:

    1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
    2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute.
    3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese.
    4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta, tossing to combine. Remove from heat.
    5. Quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, adding a bit of reserved pasta water if needed to achieve a creamy consistency.
    6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

  • Chicken Stir-Fry

    Ingredients

    • 1 lb (450g) chicken breast, sliced thinly
    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
    • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
    • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
    • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

    Instructions:

    1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
    2. Add the chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
    3. In the same skillet, add garlic and ginger. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
    4. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry until just tender.
    5. Return the chicken to the skillet. Stir in soy sauce and hoisin sauce.
    6. Add the cornstarch mixture and cook until the sauce thickens.
    7. Serve over cooked rice or noodles.

  • Vegetable Soup

    Ingredients

    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 3 carrots, sliced
    • 2 celery stalks, chopped
    • 1 cup green beans, cut into pieces
    • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
    • 4 cups vegetable broth
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:

    1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
    2. Add the carrots and celery, cooking for an additional 5 minutes.
    3. Stir in green beans, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth.
    4. Add dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
    5. Adjust seasoning as needed and serve hot.

  • Classic Caesar Salad

    Ingredients

    • 1 head Romaine lettuce, chopped
    • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Croutons
    • 1 clove garlic, minced

    Instructions:

    1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped Romaine lettuce and garlic.
    2. Toss with Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
    3. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and top with croutons.
    4. Serve immediately.
